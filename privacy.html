Privacy Policy

Effective: July 22, 2026

This policy describes how Apropos Group LLC handles information associated with National Corporate Contract Exchange. Actual data practices remain aligned with the platform’s deployed technical configuration.

1. Information you provide

The platform may process business profile information such as business name, selected capabilities, service states, business roles, capacity, contact information, and opportunity preferences when you voluntarily provide them.

2. Information collected automatically

The website and its hosting, security, and operational providers may process technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, referring page, pages visited, timestamps, error logs, and security events.

3. Browser-session storage

The free Business DNA and dashboard flow stores profile and session information in the current browser session. Closing the browser session or clearing browser data may remove that information. Saved opportunity selections in the free flow are also session-based.

4. How information is used

Information may be used to operate the platform, create a Business DNA profile, match opportunities, produce Analyze Fit reports, provide support, maintain security, diagnose errors, measure performance, and comply with legal obligations.

5. Service providers

Apropos Group LLC may use providers supporting hosting, databases, email delivery, artificial-intelligence processing, security, and platform operations. Information is shared only as reasonably necessary for those services, legal compliance, safety, or business operations. Personal information is not sold for money.

6. Public procurement links

When you follow a link to an external procurement platform, that organization’s privacy practices govern your activity there.

7. Retention and security

Information is retained only as reasonably necessary for operational, contractual, security, and legal purposes. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Your choices

You may limit information you submit, clear browser data, stop using the service, or request assistance regarding information associated with your use through an official Apropos Group LLC contact channel.

9. Children

The service is intended for business and professional use and is not directed to children under 13.

10. Changes

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in the service, law, or operational practices. The effective date will be revised when material changes are posted.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or requests may be submitted through the official Apropos Group LLC support or contact channel published on this website.
