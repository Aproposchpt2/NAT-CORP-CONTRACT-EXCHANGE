Privacy Policy

Effective: July 20, 2026

This policy describes how Apropos Group LLC handles information associated with National Corporate Contract Exchange. Actual data practices must remain aligned with the platform’s deployed technical configuration.

1. Information you provide

The platform may process business profile information such as selected service categories, keywords, business characteristics, contact information, and opportunity preferences when you voluntarily provide them.

2. Information collected automatically

The website or its hosting, security, analytics, and operational providers may process technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, referring page, pages visited, timestamps, error logs, and security events.

3. Local browser storage

Some profile or session information may be stored in your browser through local storage or similar technologies to support dashboard access and preferences. Clearing browser data may remove locally stored information or access state.

4. How information is used

Information may be used to operate the platform, create and maintain a business profile, match opportunities, improve relevance, provide support, maintain security, diagnose errors, measure performance, and comply with legal obligations.

5. Sharing

Apropos Group LLC may share information with service providers that support hosting, databases, analytics, communications, security, or platform operations; when required by law; to protect rights and safety; or as part of a business transaction. Personal information is not sold for money.

6. Public procurement links

When you follow a link to an external procurement platform, that organization’s privacy practices govern your activity there.

7. Retention and security

Information is retained only as reasonably necessary for operational, contractual, security, and legal purposes. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Your choices

You may limit information you submit, clear locally stored browser data, stop using the service, or request assistance regarding information associated with your use through the official support channel.

9. Children

The service is intended for business and professional use and is not directed to children under 13.

10. Policy changes

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in the service, law, or operational practices. The effective date will be revised when material changes are posted.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or requests may be submitted through the official Apropos Group LLC support or contact channel published on this website.
