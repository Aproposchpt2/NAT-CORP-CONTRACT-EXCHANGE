Accessibility Statement

Updated: July 22, 2026

Apropos Group LLC is committed to providing a usable digital experience for people with disabilities and to improving accessibility as the platform evolves.

Our target

National Corporate Contract Exchange is being developed with the goal of supporting WCAG 2.2 Level AA practices, including keyboard access, visible focus indicators, readable contrast, semantic headings, descriptive controls, responsive layouts, and accessible form feedback.

Current limitations

Some dashboard, intake, third-party procurement, or dynamically generated content may not yet fully meet the target. External procurement websites are controlled by their respective organizations and may have different accessibility practices.

Assistance

If you encounter a barrier, include the page, feature, device, browser, assistive technology, and a description of the problem when contacting the official Apropos Group LLC support channel. Reasonable efforts will be made to provide the information or service through an accessible alternative.

Ongoing improvement

Accessibility is reviewed during design, development, testing, and content updates. This statement will be revised as the platform’s conformance status is formally evaluated.
