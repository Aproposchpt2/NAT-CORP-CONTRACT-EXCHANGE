From scattered postings to a focused shortlist.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers before you begin.

Is contract discovery free?

Yes. Current access to the contract-discovery and opportunity-matching experience does not require a subscription or payment card. Separate professional services may have fees.

Does the platform include every government contract?

No. Coverage varies based on supported publishers, source availability, technical access, publication practices, and update schedules.

Are the opportunity listings official?

The platform is an independent discovery service. Users should review the complete solicitation, amendments, deadlines, and submission instructions on the issuing organization’s official or authorized procurement source.

Does a match mean my business is eligible?

No. Matching helps organize potentially relevant opportunities. The business remains responsible for verifying licensing, certifications, registrations, experience, capacity, eligibility, and all solicitation requirements.

Is National Corporate Contract Exchange a government agency?

No. It is an independent procurement-intelligence service developed and operated by Apropos Group LLC.
