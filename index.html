Clear answers before you begin.

Is the site free?

Yes. Intake, Business Intake, live opportunity matching, and Analyze Fit do not require a subscription or payment card. Separate professional services may have fees.

Do I need an account or code?

No. There is no member login or one-time-password flow. Every visit begins with Intake.

Does the site remember returning visitors?

No. A later visit begins again at Intake. The core flow does not restore a saved browser session.

Does the platform include every contract?

No. Coverage varies by supported publisher, source availability, publication practices, and update schedules.

Does a match guarantee eligibility or award?

No. Businesses must verify all solicitation requirements on the issuing organization’s official source.
