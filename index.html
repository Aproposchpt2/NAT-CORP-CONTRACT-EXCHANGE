From discovery to decision

Find the opportunity. Then decide whether it's worth pursuing.

Analyze Fit

Understand the opportunity before you commit.

Analyze Fit provides a deeper evaluation of a selected contract opportunity. The analysis may consider capability or service alignment, eligibility requirements, certifications, set-aside considerations, scope, deadlines, execution risk, and overall pursuit readiness.

Professional Support

Ready to compete? We can help you prepare.

Businesses that decide to pursue an opportunity may request professional Contract Proposal Development services. This is a separate professional service designed to support the proposal-development process when you are ready to compete.

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers about access and support.

What can I access for free?

National Corporate Contract Exchange provides free access to government contract discovery and opportunity-matching tools nationwide. Licensed businesses receive matches aligned with a service-based profile for relevant state and local opportunities.

Do I need a subscription to search for government contracts?

No. National Corporate Contract Exchange's contract discovery and matching are available without a subscription.

What is Analyze Fit?

Analyze Fit provides a deeper evaluation of a contract opportunity so you can better understand how it aligns with your business before committing significant time and resources.

What happens when I find a contract I want to pursue?

You can review the opportunity, use Analyze Fit to understand the alignment and requirements, and then decide whether to move forward. Businesses that choose to compete may also access professional Contract Proposal Development services.
